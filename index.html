About Us

Empowering Tribal Communities Through Infrastructure Development

Developing Infrastructure for Tribal Welfare

The Manipur Tribal Infrastructure Development Council (MTIDC) is a specialized agency under the Government of Manipur dedicated to the comprehensive development of infrastructure in tribal areas of the state.

We own, construct, and maintain public properties including government buildings, roads, bridges, and community centers with a special focus on tribal regions. Our mission is to bridge the infrastructure gap and ensure equitable development across all tribal areas of Manipur.

Addressing the citizen's grievances regarding infrastructure assets, their usage and condition is one of the key areas of focus of the Council, which is essential for effective governance and tribal empowerment.

Our Vision & Mission

Committed to Excellence in Tribal Infrastructure

Vision

To have a sustainable, efficient, safe and internationally comparable quality of public buildings, bridges, and road infrastructure in tribal areas to achieve enhanced connectivity, quick mobility, and accelerated socio-economic development of tribal communities.

Mission

  • Development of buildings, roads, and infrastructure in tribal areas for public interest
  • Enhanced connectivity for remote, far-flung and isolated tribal areas
  • Development of road networks into internationally acceptable standards
  • Establish arrangements for review of infrastructure requirements
  • Improving quality of life in tribal communities through better infrastructure
